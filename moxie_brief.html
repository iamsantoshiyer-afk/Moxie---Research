01 — The Problem
Why products stop
working after 3 months.

The most dangerous consumer signal isn't "bad product." It's "this worked and then stopped." Hair fall and breakage complaints spike 3–6 months into CGM adoption across every Indian brand. Consumers blame products. The real culprit is two compounding environmental forces that no Indian brand has formulated against — simultaneously.

Indian textured hair consumers experience a compounding cycle of hard water mineral buildup and humidity-driven cuticle disruption that makes products progressively less effective. Every Indian curly brand formulates for either cleansing or styling — nobody addresses the environmental barrier damage sitting between these two steps. Consumers blame products. The water is the problem.

Brand Positioning Matrix — Formulation Depth vs. Community Trust
